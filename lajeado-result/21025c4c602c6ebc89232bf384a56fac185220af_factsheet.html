LCZ map & Accuracy

The final filtered LCZ map is produced using all training areas and input features. Corresponding overall accuracies are:

  • OA: 0.46
  • OAu: 0.42
  • OAbu: 0.77
  • OAw: 0.85
Underlying and additional datafiles: original training areas, geotif, kmz, average confusion matrix over all bootstraps, boxplot figure with accuracies, and suspicious training areas (polygons / points).
